**Viewport Navigation and Camera Controls**

- **Zoom In/Out:**  
  - Use the **Scroll Wheel** on your mouse.  
  - Or hold **Alt (Option) + Right Mouse Button (RMB)** and drag to zoom.

- **Orbit (Rotate View):**  
  - Hold **Alt + Left Mouse Button (LMB)** and drag to orbit around the viewport center.

- **Pan:**  
  - Hold the **Middle Mouse Button (MMB)** and drag to pan the view.

- **Move Camera (WASD or Arrow Keys):**  
  - Hold **RMB** and press:  
    - **W** or **Up Arrow** to move forward  
    - **S** or **Down Arrow** to move backward  
    - **A** or **Left Arrow** to move left  
    - **D** or **Right Arrow** to move right  
    - **Q** or **Page Up** to move up  
    - **E** or **Page Down** to move down  
  - Hold **Shift** with these to move at double speed.  
  - Hold **Ctrl** with these to move at half speed.

- **Pivot Camera:**  
  - Hold **RMB** and move mouse to pivot (look around).

**Object Selection and Focus**

- **Select Object:** Left Mouse Button (LMB) click.  
- **Deselect All:** Press **Escape**.  
- **Focus on Selected Object:** Select object(s) and press **F**.  
- **Focus on All Objects:** Deselect all and press **F**.

**Asset Manipulation**

- **Delete Selected Asset:** Press **Delete** key.  
- **Duplicate Asset:** Press **Ctrl + D**.  
- **Group Selected Assets:** Press **Ctrl + G**.  
- **Toggle Visibility of Selected Asset:** Press **H**.

**Additional Useful Shortcuts**

- **Show/Hide Grid and HUD:** Shift + H  
- **Toggle UI Visibility:** F7  
- **Toggle Full Screen:** F11  
- **Capture Screenshot:** F10
